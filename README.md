# ⚖️ S2 – Smart Legal AI Agent System

🥇 Hackathon Winner – First Prize (Gold)

---

## 🚀 About
Built during a hackathon with a focus on real-world impact, not as a learning/demo project.

S2 is a safety-first legal AI system designed to reduce human error, prevent invalid legal actions, and improve decision reliability.

---

## 🧠 Key Idea
A modular AI system that:
- Assists in legal research and document analysis  
- Guides procedures with deadlines & validation  
- Monitors compliance and risks  
- Generates petitions intelligently  
- Uses a Self-Doubt Engine to avoid unsafe decisions  

---

## 🎯 Focus
> Built for accuracy, reliability, and safety in legal workflows — not just automation.
